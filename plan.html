CP1406 - Assignment 1 - Project Plan

Name: Project Plan - Slammers Beach VolleyBall.

Goals:

To increase revenue by using a website to increase the visibility of the business on the internet, using a modern medium for both existing and new, younger clients to utilise and obtain information about the sport and business.

Success Evaluation:

The website should increase overall business revenue by 50%, measurable from the time of launch to the milestone point 6 months following launch.

Target Audience

The targeted audience is the next generation of volleyball players, within the ages of 15-30 years old. The intention is that this generation who are adopted to the mordern digital world, will increase the visibilty of the business through sharing on social media, text messaging and other digital platforms. The website shall be continually updated with announcements which would restrospecticly be shared between customers increasing hit rates. The site will also however form an informative package, where any customers, both existing and potential, can obtain further information about the sport and business.
